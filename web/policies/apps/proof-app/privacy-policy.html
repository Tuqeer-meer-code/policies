Privacy Policy

Proof App

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Introduction

Proof App is an offline-first mobile application designed to help users capture and organize personal records such as photos, videos, timestamps, and location data for personal reference. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data Collection

Proof App does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. All data including photos and videos, timestamps, location data, notes, and metadata is stored locally on your device only using encrypted local storage.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Camera to capture proof media.
  • Location to attach geographic context.
  • Storage to save proof files locally.

These permissions are used only for app functionality and never for tracking.

No Cloud Sync

  • Proof App does not require user accounts.
  • Proof App does not sync data to the cloud.
  • Proof App does not upload data to any server.
  • Proof App does not share data with third parties.

You remain the sole owner of your data.

Sharing and Export

When you choose to export or share proofs (PDF, image, or video), the data is shared only through your chosen platform (email, messaging apps, etc.). Proof App does not retain or monitor shared content.

Integrity and Metadata Disclaimer

Proof App adds timestamps, location data, and integrity hashes to help users organize and reference personal records.

Important Disclaimer

  • Proofs generated by Proof App are provided for informational and personal reference purposes only.
  • Proofs do not constitute legal evidence.
  • Proofs are not guaranteed to be admissible in legal, judicial, or regulatory proceedings.
  • Users are solely responsible for determining the suitability of any exported proof for their specific use case.

Payments

In-app purchases are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Proof App does not collect or store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

Proof App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app.
