Account Deletion

Unsent – Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps

  1. Open the Unsent app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Alternative Request

Alternatively, you can request deletion by contacting us at: tuqeer.khanzada@icloud.com

When Your Account Is Deleted

  • Your profile information is permanently deleted
  • Chat history is deleted
  • Mood entries and journal data are deleted
  • Unsent letters are deleted

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and security purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.
